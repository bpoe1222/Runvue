# Runvue Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 6th, 2026
**Last Updated:** July 6th, 2026

Runvue is a local-first Android health and fitness data application operated by **[Your Legal Name or Business Name]** (“Runvue,” “we,” “us,” or “our”).

This Privacy Policy explains how Runvue handles information when you use the Runvue mobile application.

## 1. Overview

Runvue helps you view, organize, and understand health and fitness information from Android Health Connect and from files you choose to import.

Runvue is designed to operate primarily on your device. Runvue does not require an account, does not operate a developer-controlled cloud sync service, and does not sell or rent your health information.

## 2. Health and Fitness Data We Access

With your permission, Runvue may read selected health and fitness data from Android Health Connect. The specific data available to Runvue depends on the permissions you choose to grant.

Runvue may request access to the following categories when they support an active feature in the app:

* Active calories
* Basal metabolic rate
* Body fat
* Distance
* Elevation gained
* Exercise and workout sessions
* Floors climbed
* Heart rate
* Heart rate variability
* Hydration
* Resting heart rate
* Sleep sessions and sleep stages
* Steps
* VO₂ max
* Weight
* Historical Health Connect data, if you grant historical access

Runvue uses this information to provide app features such as activity summaries, workout history, health trends, recovery insights, charts, scores, data-source information, and user-selected exports.

Runvue only accesses Health Connect data after you grant the applicable Android permissions. You can deny or revoke access at any time through Android Health Connect settings.

## 3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Runvue may store the following information locally on your device:

* Health Connect data cached for app features
* Locally calculated summaries, trends, and derived metrics
* Your settings, unit preferences, filters, goals, and onboarding status
* Imported health or fitness records
* Import logs and validation results
* Export and backup preferences
* Data-source information used to explain calculations and avoid duplicate totals

This local information is used to operate the app and make its features available to you. Runvue does not upload this information to a developer-operated server.

## 4. Imported Files and Exported Files

You may choose to import files, such as supported CSV, JSON, or Runvue backup files.

When you import a file, Runvue processes the file on your device to validate its contents, identify duplicate or unsupported records, preview the proposed changes, and add approved records to Runvue’s local storage.

You may also choose to export or back up your Runvue data. Exported files are created only at your direction. Once you save, share, upload, email, or otherwise transfer an exported file, the privacy practices of the destination, recipient, app, service, or storage provider will apply.

Runvue is not responsible for how third parties handle files that you choose to share outside the app.

## 5. Information We Do Not Collect or Share

Runvue does not intentionally:

* Require a Runvue account or login
* Sell, rent, or trade your health or fitness information
* Use health information for advertising or behavioral advertising
* Use health information to determine eligibility for employment, insurance, credit, housing, or similar decisions
* Provide your health information to data brokers
* Send your health or fitness data to a developer-controlled cloud server
* Include third-party advertising services
* Use third-party analytics services to track your health information across apps or websites

Runvue does not knowingly share your Health Connect data with third parties unless you explicitly choose to export or share your data using Android’s sharing features.

## 6. How Runvue Uses Your Information

Runvue uses locally available information to:

* Display health, activity, sleep, recovery, workout, and trend information
* Calculate app metrics, summaries, and charts
* Distinguish or reduce duplicate data from multiple sources
* Support user preferences, goals, filters, and settings
* Import, restore, export, and back up data at your direction
* Diagnose and prevent app errors occurring on your device
* Improve the reliability of local app functions

Runvue does not use your health information to make medical decisions about you.

## 7. Your Choices and Controls

You control whether Runvue can access Health Connect data.

You may:

* Grant, deny, or revoke Health Connect permissions through Android Health Connect settings
* Remove imported records through Runvue’s Settings
* Clear Runvue’s local Health Connect cache, derived metrics, and sync metadata through Settings
* Delete all Runvue data stored on your device through Settings
* Export your locally stored Runvue data
* Reset Runvue, including local settings and onboarding status, through the app’s data controls

Deleting Runvue’s local data does not delete records owned by Health Connect or records created by other apps, such as Garmin, Oura, Fitbit, Samsung Health, Google Fit, or similar services.

Deleting the Runvue app may remove app-private data stored on your device, but it may not remove files you exported, files stored outside the app, or records maintained by Health Connect or other health and fitness apps.

## 8. Data Retention

Runvue retains local data on your device until you delete it through Runvue’s Settings, remove it through Android settings, uninstall the app, or otherwise delete the relevant files or records.

Imported records and app-generated calculations remain on your device until you remove them. Health Connect data remains subject to the retention practices of Android Health Connect and the apps or devices that originally created that data.

## 9. Data Security

Runvue is designed to minimize unnecessary data transfer by processing health and fitness information locally on your device.

No system can be guaranteed completely secure. You are responsible for protecting access to your device, Android account, Health Connect settings, exported files, and any destination where you choose to share your data.

## 10. Third-Party Services

Runvue works with Android Health Connect, which is provided and managed by Google and Android. Health Connect permissions and data handling are also subject to Google’s and Android’s applicable policies and settings.

Runvue may provide links to Android settings, Health Connect settings, or other third-party destinations. Those third parties operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

If this Privacy Policy is hosted online, the website hosting provider may process limited technical information, such as IP address, browser information, and server logs, to operate and secure the website. That website-hosting activity is separate from the Runvue mobile app’s local health-data processing.
